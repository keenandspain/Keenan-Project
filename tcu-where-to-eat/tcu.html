1.
Ol' South Pancake House
1509 S University Dr, Fort Worth, TX 76107
This is an old iconic restaurant in Fort Worth, built and established in 1962. We had such a great and attentive waitress, Nicole! She made the whole dining experience so pleasant. I had the New Orleans Eggs Benedict, which was delicious! But they are famous for their German pancakes and Dutch Babies, which is a smaller version of the big German pancake. This place is open 24 hours and serves breakfast 24 hours!
recommendation: German pancakes and Dutch Babies.

Raising Cane's

I go to this outstanding place often because I love the good air that is characterizes this restaurant. The staff is very friendly and professional. The service is deeply rapid. The fries and chicken strips are really good. great customer service. The staff is amazing. I would definitely recommend the food here. Oh and the music playing is good.

5.
Torchy's Tacos
928 Northton St, Fort Worth, TX 76104
First time for me. GREAT green chili queso!! So, so good! Very, creative menu options. Funky, kinda hip atmosphere. There mascot is a cute, little, red devil. I also really liked their guacamole! I can't wait to go back! Curious of their menu? Just look it up online and see for yourself! There seems to be a little something for everyone!
recommendation: green chili queso.
